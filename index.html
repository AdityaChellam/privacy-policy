Privacy Policy

This privacy policy applies to the Spin the Bottle app (the "Application") for Android devices, provided by Aditya Chellam (the "Service Provider"). The Application is intended for use "AS IS".

Overview

The Application is designed to work offline. It does not show advertisements, use analytics SDKs, or require an account. The Application does not connect to the Service Provider's servers.

Information stored on your device

The Application saves settings and gameplay preferences locally on your device, including for example:

  • Round count and spin settings (such as speed, sound, and haptic feedback)
  • Player names and optional party-game preferences
  • Onboarding and tutorial completion flags
  • An optional custom spinner image you choose from your device (stored in the app's private storage)

This information stays on your device unless you clear app data, uninstall the Application, or use your device's backup features (if enabled).

Photos and the system picker

If you choose a custom spinner image, the Application uses Android's system photo picker. The Service Provider does not receive that image over the internet; it is copied only into the Application's private storage on your device.

What we do not collect

The Application does not collect precise location (GPS), contacts, or personal information for advertising. The Application does not include third-party ad or analytics libraries and does not request network access for its core features.

Legal disclosure

The Service Provider may disclose information only if required by law, to protect rights and safety, or as described in this policy.

Children

The Application is not directed at children under 13. The Service Provider does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information, contact codeofcoffeehelp@gmail.com.

Security

The Service Provider uses reasonable safeguards for information stored in connection with the Application. No method of storage is 100% secure.

Changes

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. The Service Provider will post the updated policy on this page. Continued use after changes means you accept the updated policy.
